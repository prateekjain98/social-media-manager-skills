Interview Prep
10 Things You Should Never
Say in a Job Interview
One wrong sentence can create the wrong impression
1
"I hated my previous boss"
Sounds negative and unprofessional
2
"I just need any job"
Shows lack of direction
3
"What does your company do?"
Signals poor preparation
4
"I'm not sure I can do this"
Undermines your confidence
5
"It's on my resume"
Comes across as dismissive
6
"I'm here only for the salary"
Value and growth come first
7
"I don't have any weaknesses"
Sounds unrealistic
8
"Sorry, I'm late"
Punctuality = professionalism
9
"How soon can I take leave?"
Signals lack of commitment
10
"I don't have any questions"
Shows low engagement
Replace these with confident, positive responses. Always ask something thoughtful.
