## Colors (GitHub Dark palette, WCAG AA compliant)
- BG: `#0D1117` | Card: `#161B22` | Card border: `#30363D`
- Text primary: `#F0F6FC` (15.3:1) | Secondary: `#C9D1D9` (10.5:1) | Tertiary: `#8B949E` (5.5:1)
- Accent red: `#F85149` | Green: `#3FB950` | Blue: `#58A6FF`
- Dividers: 1px `#30363D` (never solid colored lines)

## Typography (at 1x, multiply by S for supersampling)
- Hero stat: 72px | Title: 42px | Subtitle: 28px | Section: 24px
- Body large: 20px | Body: 18px | Caption: 16px (MINIMUM)
- Line height: headings 1.2, body 1.5
- Max 2 font families. All spacing multiples of 8.

## Spacing (8px grid)
- Margins: 64px horizontal, 48px vertical
- Content area: 1072 x 579px
- Gaps: xs=8, sm=16, md=24, lg=32, xl=48, xxl=64
- Border radius: 8px everywhere

## Rules
- 40-50% whitespace
- Max 5 accent colors (1 dominant, 1 secondary, rest supporting)
- Never pure #000 or #FFF
- Safe zone: center 80% for LinkedIn, center 60-70% for X cropping
- 2x supersampling + LANCZOS downscale
