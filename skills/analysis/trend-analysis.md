# Trend Analysis

Analyze and leverage current social media trends.

## Skill: `/trend-analysis`

**CRITICAL: Read `_core-principles.md` first. Content ideas for trends must be human-authentic. Never reveal AI identity.**

---

## Human-Authenticity Rules for Trend Analysis

### Suggest Human-Native Trend Participation
- Trend adaptations should sound natural for the brand
- Ideas should feel like a real creator thought of them
- Don't force trends—suggest authentic fits only
- Include personality in how to approach trends
- Sound like an insider, not an analyst

### AVOID Robot Trend Advice
- "Consider participating in this trend"
- Generic angle suggestions
- Forced brand insertions into trends
- Missing the actual vibe of the trend

### DO Advise Like This
- "This trend is perfect for you because..."
- "Here's a specific spin that would actually work"
- "Skip this one—it's not your vibe"
- "The way to do this without looking cringe is..."
- Native, informed, personality-driven advice

**Challenge Trends**
- Dance challenges

- Brand challenges

**Meme Trends**
- Image memes
- Text formats

- Relatable humor

**Topic Trends**
- News-driven conversations
- Cultural moments
- Industry discussions
- Seasonal trends

### 4. Trend Evaluation Criteria

**Should You Jump on a Trend?**

| Factor | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Relevance | Does it fit your brand/niche? |
| Timing | Are you early enough? |
| Authenticity | Can you do it genuinely? |
| Value | Does it serve your audience? |
| Capability | Do you have resources to execute? |
| Risk | Any brand safety concerns? |

**Trend Timing:**
- Emerging (Days 1-3): Highest potential, some risk
- Peak (Days 4-7): Still valuable, more crowded
- Declining (Days 8+): Often too late, unless adding unique spin

### 5. Trend Sources

**Where to Find Trends:**
- TikTok: For You Page, Discover, Creative Center
- Instagram: Reels tab, Explore, Creator accounts
- Twitter: Trending, Moments, industry accounts
- LinkedIn: News, Trending articles
- Google Trends
- Industry newsletters
- Competitor monitoring

### 6. Brand Safety Considerations

**Before Participating, Check:**
- Is the trend's origin appropriate?
- Are there any controversial associations?
- Could participation be seen as insensitive?
- Does it align with brand values?
- Is timing appropriate (current events)?
