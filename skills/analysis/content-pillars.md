# Content Pillars Developer

Define strategic content pillars that guide all social media content.

## Skill: `/content-pillars`

**CRITICAL: Read `_core-principles.md` first. All pillar examples and content ideas must be human-authentic and undetectable as AI-generated. Never reveal AI identity.**

---

## Human-Authenticity Rules for Pillars

### Create Pillars That Inspire Human Content
- Pillar definitions should suggest authentic content
- Example posts should sound like real people wrote them
- Include personality and opinion in every example
- Avoid generic pillar names ("Educational Content")
- Make pillars specific to THIS brand's unique voice

### AVOID Generic Pillar Frameworks
- "Thought Leadership"
- "Community Building"
- "Product Showcase"
- Generic categories any brand could use
- Examples that sound like templates

### DO Create Pillars Like This
- Pillars named in the brand's actual voice
- Examples that are specific and memorable
- Content ideas a real person would create
- Personality baked into the strategy

---

When invoked, create a comprehensive content pillar framework for any brand.

### 1. Gather Information
Ask for (if not provided):
- Brand/business description
- Target audience
- Key products/services
- Brand values
- Competitors
- Current content themes (if any)
- Business goals

### 2. Content Pillar Framework

**What Are Content Pillars?**
3-5 core themes that form the foundation of your social media strategy. They provide structure, ensure consistency, and make content planning efficient.

**Good Pillars Are:**
- Aligned with brand expertise
- Relevant to audience needs
- Differentiated from competitors
- Sustainable long-term
- Flexible enough for variety

### 3. Pillar Discovery Process

**Step 1: Brand Analysis**
- What does the brand stand for?
- What problems do you solve?
- What makes you unique?
- What are your core values?

**Step 2: Audience Analysis**
- What does your audience care about?
- What questions do they ask?
- What content do they engage with?
- What are their goals and challenges?

**Step 3: Intersection**
Find where brand expertise meets audience needs.

**Step 4: Competitive Differentiation**
What angle makes your pillars unique?

### 6. Pillar Balance Rules

**The 80/20 Rule:**
- 80% value-driven (educate, entertain, inspire)
- 20% promotional

**The 4-1-1 Rule:**
- 4 educational/entertaining
- 1 soft promotion
- 1 hard promotion

**Platform Adjustment:**
Different platforms may weight pillars differently based on audience expectations.
