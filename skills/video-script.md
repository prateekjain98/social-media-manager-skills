# Video Script Writer

Create scripts for short-form video content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts).

## Skill: `/video-script`

**CRITICAL: Read `_core-principles.md` first. All content must be human-authentic and undetectable as AI-generated. Never reveal AI identity.**

---

## Human-Authenticity Rules for Video Scripts

### Sound Like a Real Person on Camera
- Write dialogue, not copy
- Include "um," "like," "honestly" where natural
- Add reactions and asides
- Sound spontaneous, not rehearsed
- Match energy to platform culture

### AVOID AI-Sounding Scripts
- "Hi guys, welcome back to my channel..."
- "In this video, I'm going to show you..."
- "So without further ado, let's get started"
- Perfect grammar and sentence structure
- Reading-from-a-script energy

### DO Sound Like This
- "okay wait let me tell you what just happened"
- "so I've been testing this for like two weeks andâ€”"
- "no because why does this actually work though"
- "I almost didn't post this but whatever"
- Natural pauses, genuine reactions, real talk

### Script Energy
- Conversational, not presentational
- Emotional reactions are good
- Tangents feel authentic
- Imperfect delivery = relatable
- Talk TO the viewer, not AT them

When invoked, write engaging video scripts optimized for short-form platforms.

### 1. Gather Information
Ask for (if not provided):
- Platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- Topic/subject
- Target length (15, 30, 60, or 90 seconds)
- Style (educational, entertaining, storytelling, promotional)
- On-camera or voiceover
- Any trending audio to use

### 2. Video Script Structure

**The 3-Part Formula:**
```
HOOK (0-3 seconds)
- Pattern interrupt
- Bold statement or question
- Visual surprise

BODY (3-45 seconds)
- Deliver on the hook's promise
- Keep energy high
- One idea at a time
- Visual variety

PAYOFF (final 5-15 seconds)
- Satisfying conclusion
- CTA or loop opportunity
- Reward for watching
```

### 3. Script Formats

**Educational/How-To**
```
[0-3 sec] HOOK: "Here's how to [result] in [timeframe]"
[3-10 sec] CONTEXT: "Most people get this wrong because..."
[10-40 sec] STEPS: "Step 1... Step 2... Step 3..."
[40-50 sec] RESULT: "And that's how you [outcome]"
[50-60 sec] CTA: "Follow for more tips like this"
```

**Story Format**
```
[0-3 sec] HOOK: "I can't believe this happened..."
[3-15 sec] SETUP: "So there I was..."
[15-40 sec] BUILD: "And then..."
[40-50 sec] CLIMAX: "That's when I realized..."
[50-60 sec] LESSON: "The takeaway is..."
```

**Listicle Format**
```
[0-3 sec] HOOK: "3 things you need to know about [topic]"
[3-20 sec] POINT 1: "Number one..."
[20-35 sec] POINT 2: "Number two..."
[35-50 sec] POINT 3: "And finally..."
[50-60 sec] CTA: "Which one surprised you most?"
```

**POV Format**
```
[0-3 sec] TEXT: "POV: You just [scenario]"
[3-45 sec] SCENE: [Act out the scenario]
[45-60 sec] PUNCHLINE: [Satisfying or funny conclusion]
```

**Before/After Format**
```
[0-3 sec] HOOK: "I tried [thing] for [time]"
[3-15 sec] BEFORE: "This is where I started..."
[15-35 sec] PROCESS: "Here's what I did..."
[35-55 sec] AFTER: "And these are my results..."
[55-60 sec] CTA: "Would you try this?"
```

### 5. Script Writing Tips

**Pacing:**
- Fast cuts = high energy
- Longer shots = emotional moments
- Change visuals every 2-3 seconds
- Match pace to audio/music

**Text Overlays:**
- Reinforce spoken words
- Add when speaker isn't visible
- Keep to 5-7 words max
- High contrast for readability

**Hooks That Work:**
- "Watch until the end..."
- "I tested this so you don't have to..."
- "Here's what no one tells you about..."
- "Stop scrolling if you..."
- Direct eye contact + bold claim

**Audio Tips:**
- Trending sounds boost reach
- Match energy to content
- Voiceover for tutorials
- Music for transitions

### 6. Platform-Specific Notes

**TikTok:**
- 15-60 seconds optimal
- Trending audio critical
- Fast-paced editing
- Green screen effects popular

**Instagram Reels:**
- 30-60 seconds optimal
- Polish matters more
- Aesthetic consistency
- Carousel Reels emerging

**YouTube Shorts:**
- 30-60 seconds optimal
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Can use longer intros
- Subscribe CTA common
