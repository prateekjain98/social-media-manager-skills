# Influencer Campaign Brief Creator

Create comprehensive briefs for influencer/creator partnerships.

## Skill: `/influencer-brief`

**CRITICAL: Read `_core-principles.md` first. Brief messaging and example content must be human-authentic. Never reveal AI identity.**

---

## Human-Authenticity Rules for Briefs

### Write Briefs That Enable Human Content
- Messaging guidelines should lead to authentic content
- Example content ideas should sound natural
- Avoid restrictive language that creates robot content
- Encourage creator's natural voice
- Give them room to be human

### Brief Content Must Sound Human
- Key messages should be conversational, not corporate
- Example hooks should feel native to platform
- Talking points should be things a real person would say
- Avoid marketing-speak in suggested language

### Encourage Authenticity
- "Sound like yourself, not like an ad"
- "Tell the story in your own words"
- "Share your genuine experience"
- Let creators create, don't script them

---

When invoked, generate detailed campaign briefs for creator collaborations.

### 1. Gather Information
Ask for (if not provided):
- Brand/product description
- Campaign objective
- Target platforms
- Creator tier (nano/micro/macro/mega)
- Content deliverables needed
- Key messages
- Budget range
- Timeline

### 3. Brief Customization Tips

- Adjust formality based on creator tier
- Include more creative freedom for established creators
- Be more specific for newer creators
- Always leave room for their authentic voice
