## X Algorithm Intelligence (2026)

### How the Algorithm Works
1. **75% algorithmic**: Most views come from "For You" not Following
2. **Reply Priority**: Replies/conversations weighted heavily
3. **Engagement Velocity**: Fast engagement = wider reach
4. **Network Effects**: Who engages matters (influential accounts boost more)
5. **Content Type**: Native content favored over links

### Ranking Signals (In Order of Importance)
1. **Replies**: Highest weightâ€”sparks conversation
2. **Retweets/Reposts**: Sharing signals value
3. **Likes**: Basic engagement signal
4. **Bookmarks**: Hidden signal for quality
5. **Dwell Time**: Time spent on tweet
6. **Profile Clicks**: Interest indicator

### Algorithm Boosters
- Getting replies from accounts with high follower counts
- Creating conversation threads (back-and-forth)
- Posting when your audience is online
- Using 1-2 hashtags (not more)
- Native images/videos
- X Premium subscription (verified accounts get boost)

### Algorithm Killers
- External links (put in reply or use link cards)
- Too many hashtags (3+ hurts reach)
- Engagement bait that gets flagged
- Posting and not replying to responses
- Identical content/format repeatedly

## Posting Strategy

### Optimal Timing
- **Best Days**: Monday-Friday
- **Best Times**: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm (audience timezone)
- **Real-time**: Breaking news/trends = post immediately

### Posting Frequency
- **Minimum**: 3 tweets/day
- **Optimal**: 5-15 tweets/day (including replies)
- **Power Users**: 20+ including engagement

### Content Mix
| Type | Percentage | Purpose |
|------|------------|---------|
| Original Takes/Insights | 30% | Build authority |
| Engagement/Replies | 30% | Build community |
| Reposts with Commentary | 20% | Stay relevant |
| Threads | 15% | Deep value |
| Promotional | 5% | Drive business |

## Engagement Strategy

### Engagement Protocol
1. Post at optimal time
2. Reply to every response for first 1-2 hours
3. Quote tweet relevant content in your niche
4. Engage with larger accounts (thoughtfully, not thirsty)
5. Join trending conversations with hot takes

### Reply Strategy (Your Comments on Others)
- Add genuine value, not "Great tweet!"
- Share a related experience or take
- Ask a thoughtful follow-up question
- Be first on popular accounts' tweets
- Build relationships through consistent engagement

### Building Your Network
- Identify 20-50 accounts in your niche
- Engage with them daily
- Create "mutuals" through consistent interaction
- Reply to their replies
- Share their content with genuine commentary
