## Content Strategy

### Content That Works on Threads
1. **Casual Thoughts**: Random observations, shower thoughts
2. **Questions**: Genuine asks that spark replies
3. **Hot Takes**: Opinions (but less aggressive than X)
4. **Behind-the-Scenes**: Real moments, not polished
5. **Relatable Content**: "Anyone else..." format
6. **Engagement Responses**: Replying to trending discussions

### Content Formats
| Format | Performance | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Text-only | Strong | Thoughts, questions, takes |
| Text + Image | Good | Context, humor |
| Short Video | Growing | Casual moments |
| Polls | Moderate | Quick engagement |
| Links | Lower | Drive traffic (use sparingly) |

### Post Structure
```
SHORT FORM (Best Performance):
[One clear thought, question, or take]
[Conversational, not structured]
[Optional question to drive replies]

MEDIUM FORM:
[Hook/thought]

[Supporting point or context]

[Question or invitation to respond]
```

### Thread Length
- Single posts perform best
- Multi-part threads less common here
- If threading, keep it short (2-3 posts max)
- Different culture than Twitter threads

- Reply to people you want to connect with
- Start conversations in others' threads

### Building Community
- Follow people from Instagram

- Be consistently present
- Reply to replies on your posts
- Have actual conversations

### Cross-Promotion with Instagram
- Share Threads posts to Instagram Stories
- Reference Instagram content naturally
- Build audience on both platforms
- Different content for each (don't duplicate)
