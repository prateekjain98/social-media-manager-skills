## Instagram Algorithm Intelligence (2026)

### Multiple Algorithms, Not One
Instagram uses DIFFERENT algorithms for:
1. **Feed**: Personalized based on relationships + interests
2. **Stories**: Prioritizes accounts you interact with most
3. **Reels**: Discovery-focused, content > relationships
4. **Explore**: Interest-based discovery

### Feed Algorithm Signals
1. **Relationship**: How often you interact with this account
2. **Interest**: Predicted interest based on past behavior
3. **Timeliness**: Newer posts weighted higher
4. **Session**: Length of time user spends on app

### Reels Algorithm Signals (Most Important for Growth)
1. **Shares**: THE most weighted signal (saves relationships)
2. **Watch Time**: Completion rate and rewatch
3. **Likes/Comments**: Engagement signals
4. **Audio Usage**: Using trending audio helps discovery
5. **Quality Signals**: Resolution, lighting, etc.

### Stories Algorithm
- Prioritizes accounts you engage with most
- Interactive elements (polls, questions) boost engagement
- Consistent stories keep you top of feed
- First 3 story slots most valuable

### Explore Algorithm
- Based on content similarity to what you engage with
- Strong engagement signals = Explore page potential
- Hashtags and keywords influence discovery

## Content Strategy by Format

### Reels (Highest Reach Potential)
```
OPTIMAL LENGTH: 30-60 seconds (up to 90 for high retention)
POSTING FREQUENCY: 4-7 per week minimum

STRUCTURE:
Hook (0-3s): Stop the scroll
Content (3-50s): Deliver value/entertainment
Payoff (final): Resolution + loop potential

KEY METRICS:
- Average watch time: 50%+ ideal
- Share rate: Most important
- Saves: Second most important
- Comments: Third
- Likes: Fourth
```

### Carousel Posts (Second Highest Reach)
```
OPTIMAL SLIDES: 5-10 slides
ENGAGEMENT RATE: 2x single images

STRUCTURE:
Slide 1: Hook/headline that stops scroll
Slide 2: Problem or setup
Slides 3-8: Main content (one idea per slide)
Slide 9: Summary or recap
Slide 10: CTA

DESIGN:
- Consistent visual style
- Large, readable text
- Swipe indicator on slide 1
- Each slide has value (partial scroll = engagement)
```

### Single Image Posts
```
USE FOR: Announcements, photography, statements
OPTIMAL: High-quality, scroll-stopping visuals

PAIR WITH:
- Strong caption (mini-blog format works)
- Clear CTA or question
- Relevant hashtags
```

### Stories
```
FREQUENCY: Daily, multiple per day
OPTIMAL FORMAT: Mix of:
- Behind-the-scenes
- Polls and questions
- Quick updates
- Link sharing (for accounts with access)
- Reposts and shares

INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS:
- Polls, Questions, Quizzes boost engagement
- Slider reactions increase interaction
- Countdown for announcements
- Links for driving traffic
```

## Engagement Strategy

### Engagement Windows
- **First 30 minutes**: Critical for algorithm signal
- **First hour**: Stay engaged, reply to all comments
- **First 24 hours**: Continue engagement, share to stories

### Comment Strategy
- Reply to every comment with substance
- Ask follow-up questions to drive more replies
- Pin best comments for social proof
- Heart/like comments at minimum

### DM Strategy
- Respond to DMs promptly
- Use voice messages for personal touch
- Create automated responses for FAQs (transparently)
- Move conversations to DMs for deeper connection

### Building Engagement
- Engage with others BEFORE posting (15-30 min)
- Comment on accounts in your niche
- Respond to stories with genuine messages
- Create "Share to Story" worthy content
- Use "Add Yours" stickers to increase UGC

## Instagram-Specific Tips

### Profile Optimization
- **Username**: Searchable, memorable
- **Name**: Include keywords for search
- **Bio**: Value prop + personality + CTA (150 chars)
- **Highlights**: Organized, on-brand covers
- **Link**: Link-in-bio tool for multiple destinations

### 2026 Instagram Trends
- **Reels dominate reach**: Video-first strategy essential
- **Shares > Saves > Comments**: New engagement hierarchy
- **Authenticity over polish**: Real moments perform
- **SEO matters**: Keywords in captions, alt text
- **DMs drive connection**: Private engagement valued

### What Works
- Face on camera (especially for Reels)
- Trending audio with unique spin
- Educational content that gets saved
- Shareable content (quotes, tips, humor)
- Consistent aesthetic with personality
- Behind-the-scenes and raw moments
- Engaging in first hour after posting

### What Doesn't Work
- Posting without engaging
- Same content type repeatedly
- Ignoring Reels (massive reach penalty)
- Buying followers/engagement
- Watermarked reposts from TikTok
- Over-polished, inauthentic content
- Hard selling in every post
- Inconsistent posting schedule
