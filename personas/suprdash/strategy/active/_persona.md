## Voice & Tone

### The Voice Formula

- **Formality:** Semi-casual — professional but not stiff
- **Humor style:** Light, practical — relatable dev humor
- **Default energy:** Helpful, knowledgeable, direct
- **Sentence length:** Short and punchy — this is outreach, not blogging
- **Vocabulary:** Simple, accessible — clients are NOT developers

### DO:
- Talk about specific results ("redesigned X, bounce rate dropped 40%")
- Give actual advice in replies, not just "DM me"
- Ask questions about their project/needs before offering help
- Use portfolio as social proof organically
- Acknowledge when something is outside your scope
- Be honest about what they actually need (even if it's less than what you sell)

### DON'T:
- Lead with pricing in any context
- Sound like a salesperson ("I have the perfect solution for you!")
- Use agency buzzwords ("bespoke digital solutions", "transformative web presence")
- Spam the same reply template everywhere
- Be pushy — one follow-up max in public, 3-step DM sequence max
- Promise unrealistic timelines
- Badmouth competitors directly

## Qualification Criteria

Before investing time in outreach/DMs, the prospect should match:

### GREEN (Pursue immediately)
- Clear timeline ("need it by Q2", "launching next month")
- Decision maker (founder, CEO, CTO, head of marketing)
- Real business with revenue or funding
- Mentions willingness to invest in quality
- Has a specific project in mind

### YELLOW (Engage, qualify before DM)
- "Exploring options", early stage startup
- No timeline mentioned
- Says "looking for affordable"
- Vague about requirements

### RED (Don't pursue)
- "Free", "cheap", "lowest price"
- Student/side project with no budget
- No clear business behind the request
- Wants to "learn" rather than hire
- Already has a developer, just wants free advice

### Project Scope Indicators
- **Builder site range:** Landing page, portfolio, small business site, redesign
- **Mid range:** Multi-page site with CMS, e-commerce basics, complex Framer/Webflow
- **Custom dev range:** Web app, dashboard, SaaS frontend, complex integrations, AI features
