## Content Strategy Overhaul

### 1. Threads (2-3 per week) — TOP PRIORITY

Threads are the single highest-leverage content for follower growth. Each thread should:
- Have a strong hook tweet (under 280 chars, curiosity gap)
- Be 5-8 tweets long
- End with a CTA (follow for more, drop a like if useful)
- Include 1 specific number or data point per tweet

**Thread topics (rotate weekly):**
1. "How we built BoloSign to compete with DocuSign as a 5-person team" (founder story)
2. "What I learned running AI in production for 6 months" (AI in production)
3. "The SaaS pricing playbook incumbents don't want you to know" (SaaS insights)
4. "From BITS Pilani to 2x SaaS exits: what actually matters" (personal story)
5. "5 things I'd do differently building my first SaaS" (lessons learned)

### 2. Quote Tweets (3-5 per day)

QTs get 5-10x more visibility than replies. Every session should include:
- 2-3 QTs with a strong take added
- Pick posts with 100-1K likes (sweet spot for QT visibility)
- Add contrarian angle, not just "great point"

### 3. Replies (15-20 per day)

Same diversification matrix but with new targets:
- Speed > perfection. Be in first 10 replies.
- Shorter replies (2-3 sentences max) for speed
- Save long thoughtful replies for Tier 2-3 accounts only

### 4. Strategic Following (20-30 per day)

**Follow-back hunting:**
- Follow accounts in 1K-10K range who are active (posted in last 48h)
- Follow people who liked/replied to posts in your niche
- Follow Indian SaaS/tech founders specifically
- Unfollow after 7 days if no follow-back (start after 1 week)

**Target categories:**
- SaaS founders tweeting about building
- AI/ML practitioners sharing real production stories
- Indian tech founders and VCs
- People who engage with accounts you follow

### 5. Original Posts (2-3 per day)

- 1 thread per session (when scheduled)
- 1-2 standalone hot takes
- At least 1 post with an image (2-3x distribution boost)
- Time posts for peak hours: 8-9 AM IST, 6-7 PM IST, 9-10 PM IST (US morning)

## Daily Session Structure (Revised)

### Morning Session (45 min) — 8-9 AM IST
1. Check notifications/mentions (5 min)
2. Like + bookmark 10-15 posts from feed (5 min)
3. Post 1 original (hot take or thread) (5 min)
4. Hunt 5-8 fresh posts (< 2hrs old) from Tier 2-3 accounts (10 min)
5. Draft + post 8-10 quick replies (15 min)
6. Follow 10-15 relevant accounts (5 min)

### Evening Session (30 min) — 6-7 PM IST
1. Check notifications, reply to mentions (5 min)
2. Post 1 original or QT (5 min)
3. Hunt 3-5 posts, draft + post 5-7 replies (15 min)
4. Follow 10-15 accounts, like 10 posts (5 min)

### Night Session (15 min) — 9-10 PM IST (US morning)
1. Post 1 QT or original timed for US audience (5 min)
2. 3-5 quick replies on US-centric posts (10 min)

## Anti-Spam Rules (Added Mar 26, 2026)

> **Context:** @GergelyOrosz blocked @chigup36 after 3 BoloSign spam replies in 3 minutes on the same post (May 29, 2025). @manol_ai also blocked for unknown reasons.

1. **Max 1 reply per person per day.** Never reply twice to the same person in a session.
2. **Max 1-in-5 replies mention BoloSign.** Product plugs must add genuine value, not just name-drop.
3. **Never reply with only product promotion.** Every reply must answer the question or add a new angle first. Product mention is secondary.
4. **No copy-paste replies.** Each reply must be unique and tailored to the specific post.
5. **Check for blocks/restrictions before replying.** If reply dialog shows "blocked" or "restricted", skip immediately.
6. **Space replies 30+ seconds apart.** Rapid-fire posting looks bot-like to both humans and algorithms.
