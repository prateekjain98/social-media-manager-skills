## Anti-Spam Rules (Learned From Blocks)

**Accounts that blocked @chigup36:**
- @GergelyOrosz — 3 BoloSign spam replies in 3 minutes on May 29, 2025
- @manol_ai — unknown reason, no reply history found

**Rules to prevent future blocks:**
1. **Max 1 reply per person per day.** Never reply twice to the same person in a session.
2. **Max 1-in-5 replies mention BoloSign.** Product plugs must add genuine value, not just name-drop.
3. **Never reply with only product promotion.** Every reply must answer the question or add a new angle first. Product mention is secondary.
4. **No copy-paste replies.** Each reply must be unique. Identical-sounding replies across threads trigger spam detection.
5. **Check for blocks before replying.** If reply dialog shows "blocked" or "restricted", move on immediately. Don't waste time.
6. **Space replies 30+ seconds apart.** Rapid-fire posting looks bot-like.
