## Content Adaptation Guide

```
X (up to 25K chars with Premium):
├── First line must hook (visual + surprising stat or contrarian take)
├── Post to Communities: Design, Startups, Build in Public, Marketing, Data/Analytics
├── Images are 2-3x boost — ALWAYS include a visual
├── Link to wireframr.io in BIO, not in post body (algorithm penalty)
├── Reply to own post with link if needed
└── Short and punchy. No walls of text.

LinkedIn (3,000 char limit):
├── Expand with more context and business use case framing
├── Professional tone but still human
├── Hashtags: #DataVisualization #Infographic #AI #MarketingTools #DesignTools
├── Company page posts (not personal — Wireframr is a product brand)
└── External links OK but engagement drops — put link in first comment

Reddit (varies):
├── NEVER post promotional content
├── Share data-viz expertise in comments only
├── If relevant, share template/tip as genuine contribution
├── No links to wireframr.io unless explicitly asked
└── Subreddits: r/dataisbeautiful, r/graphic_design, r/marketing, r/smallbusiness

Indie Hackers:
├── Build-in-public is expected and welcomed
├── Be transparent about metrics, challenges, decisions
├── Engage genuinely in comments
└── Product mention is natural here, not spammy
```
